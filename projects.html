Life Projects

CS50
2021 - 2022
CS50 is a free online course from Harvard University that teaches programming languages and web development. It is a course that teaches students to code by building projects such as in C, Python, SQL, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
This website, per example, was built as the last project of this course.

It is a 11 week course in which you are expected to work from 10-20 hours per week, which makes up to up to 220 hours in total, plus the final project.

The objective of beginning this course was to take some advance for some of my uni classes, but I ended up liking it so much that in the end I actually learned way more than what was needed.
Japan
04/2022 - 10/2022
I did a sabbatic year as an exchange student in Japan in a language school called the 大阪YMCA学院 for around 6 months.
I had already studied Japanese on my own for about 2 years, so I had the opportunity to start school in the more advanced classes.

It was an amazing experience that changed my life, and I learned more things about the culture, the language, the world and myself that I could have ever hoped for. I would love to answer questions about my experience if you have any.

During my studies there I passed the Japanese Language Proficiency test N2 (JLPT N2) which would be the equivalent of B2~C1 in CEFR terms.
